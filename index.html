About

Hi! I'm Kori. I am currently pursuing my BA in multimedia from San Diego State University paired with a minor in Computer Science. I have always been doodling or creating since I was a child, but I also have a fascination with math and emerging technologies. These combined is what led me to graphic design with UX and UI.

Outside of my studies I am obsessed with magazines (I take a lot of pride in my always growing magazine collection), fashion, cooking, curating cool outfits, painting, road trips, and the ocean. I love projecting different aesthetics through my outfits, my room design, and my art.
